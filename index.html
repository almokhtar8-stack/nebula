📡
Weekly Scan
Automated queries hit PubMed E-utilities and GWAS Catalog API every Monday at 06:00. Searches 8 trait categories matching the whitelist.
🏆
Scoring
Each candidate is scored on sample size, replication, effect size, and mechanistic plausibility. Only high-scoring candidates enter the review queue.
👨‍⚕️
Expert Review
A genetic counsellor reviews queued candidates — approve, reject, or flag for further review. Approval moves the variant to the whitelist.
✅
Whitelist Update
Approved variants enter the whitelist with evidence grade and risk allele. The rule engine updates. Users are notified their report has new findings.
