Weekly Research Scan
Every Monday, Nebula automatically scans the latest published genetic studies across nutrition, fitness, sleep, and health risk — looking for discoveries that apply to our users.

We Score What We Find
Not every study is worth acting on. We score findings by how large the study was, how many times it was replicated, and how strong the effect actually is. Only the best make the cut.

A genetic counsellor reviews every queued finding before it touches anyone's report. Approved. Rejected. Or flagged for more evidence. No automation takes this step.

When something new is approved, it enters the platform. If it applies to your DNA, your report updates — and you get notified. Your results grow smarter over time.
