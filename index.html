DETERMINISTIC ENGINE
21 IF/THEN rules. Every recommendation traces back to a specific published GWAS. No ML, no black boxes — fully auditable by any scientist.
🛡️
THREE-TIER SAFETY
Tier 1 wellness → user directly. Tier 2 risk flag → physician discussion. Tier 3 clinical → held until genetic counsellor reviews.
📈
POLYGENIC RISK SCORES
PRS for CAD, T2D, Breast Cancer, Prostate Cancer. Population percentile with ancestry mismatch detection and flagging.
💬
QUESTIONNAIRE ENGINE
40+ field lifestyle questionnaire across 6 sections. Rules use DNA and lifestyle data together — sleep, caffeine, training, family history.

📡
Weekly Scan
Automated queries hit PubMed E-utilities and GWAS Catalog API every Monday at 06:00. Searches 8 trait categories matching the whitelist.
🏆
Scoring
Each candidate is scored on sample size, replication, effect size, and mechanistic plausibility. Only candidates above threshold enter the review queue.
👨‍⚕️
Expert Review
A genetic counsellor reviews queued candidates. They can approve, reject, or flag for further review. Approval moves a variant to the whitelist.
✅
Whitelist Update
Approved variants enter the whitelist with evidence grade and risk allele. The rule engine is updated. Users are notified their report has new findings.
